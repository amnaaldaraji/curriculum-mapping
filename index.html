Data Protection and Consent — GDPR

Your rights under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

You have several rights regarding the data collected about you:

  • Access: You have the right to request access to the data processed about you.
  • Corrections: You can ask for any incorrect data to be corrected.
  • Deletion and Restriction: You may request that your data be deleted or that its processing be restricted. However, please note that the right to deletion or restriction does not apply if the data is essential for the research.

To exercise your rights, contact Tobias Alfvén at tobias.alfven@ki.se who is responsible for your personal data. The Data Protection Officer can also be reached at dataskyddsombud@ki.se. If you are dissatisfied with how your personal data is handled, you can file a complaint with the Swedish Authority for Privacy Protection.

What happens to my data?

Your participation in this survey is completely anonymous, no personal data is collected and no one will be able to identify you from your responses. The data collected will be securely stored and managed according to Karolinska Institutet's (KI) data protection guidelines. Your answers will be processed in such a way that they remain confidential and inaccessible to unauthorized persons.

How can I access the survey results?

To receive information about the study's results, please contact Jennifer Valcke at jennifer.valcke@ki.se.
